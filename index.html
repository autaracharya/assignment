King Prithvi Narayan Shah

Biography

King Prithvi Narayan Shah was the first King of unified NepalKing Prithvi Narayan Shah, born in 1723, was a visionary and influential figure in the history of Nepal. He is renowned for his strategic brilliance and leadership in uniting the fragmented kingdoms of the Himalayan region into a unified Nepal in the mid-18th century. His relentless efforts and military campaigns led to the creation of a strong and cohesive nation, and he became the first King of the Shah dynasty. King Prithvi Narayan Shah's legacy is deeply rooted in Nepal's history as the architect of its territorial integrity and sovereignty, making him a revered national hero and a symbol of Nepalese unity.
